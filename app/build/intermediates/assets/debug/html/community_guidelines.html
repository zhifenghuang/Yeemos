What we have in mind for our community:

Self-expression:

Yeemos is the place for you to express yourself. Sharing your thoughts and feelings with friends and/or people around the world becomes more meaningful when it's emotionally connected.

Be supportive:

Yeemos makes great effort to create a safe and supportive space where people help and support each other through shared feelings and emotions.

Empathy:

We want your posts on Yeemos to be meaningful to you as well as to people who can relate to your expression. We strive to create a space for you to share your thoughts and feelings to whoever you truly feel comfortable with. Sharing only becomes meaningful when people truly understand and feel related.

Privacy:

Yeemos respects the importance of your privacy, PERIOD! We believe that you should have control over how you want to interact with others. You may choose to share your emotions and everyday life with your friends on Yeemos, and we believe sometimes things are better off sharing with complete strangers as well. Now you have the options to decide who gets to see your posts.

Use of Anonymous Feature:

Sometimes people feel more comfortable sharing true feelings and thoughts without revealing own identities. We understand and respect that it is part of the human natures. In order for our community to truly express and be emotionally connected, we added the "Anonymous" feature in post settings. However, with great feature comes with great responsibility! Yeemos HAS ABSOLUTELY ZERO TOLERANCE FOR ANY OBJECTIONABLE CONTENT AND/OR ANY ABUSIVE USERS! If any user violates our community guidelines, Yeemos may delete the content and/or accounts without prior notice. Furthermore, Yeemos may inform or assist local law enforcement if further legal action is required.

What Yeemos expects from you:

Contribute according to our community values:

  • Supportive
  • Restraint

  • Respect

    • Why we have Community Guidelines:

    Whilst Yeemos encourages you to freely and truly express yourself, we also make great efforts to shape a positive and supportive community that values the wellbeing of all our users. These guidelines establish the boundaries for the content and behaviors that we expect - to avoid any confusion:

    Respect our community

    Yeemos is a place where different people from all over the globe can come to share emotions and the most personal of moments so we are unwavering in our determination to foster a respectful and understanding community. In order to preserve people's safety and promote respect, Yeemos may delete your content or accounts without prior notice if you harass, threaten, stalk or attempt to humiliate or publicly shame others or use hateful language.

    Yeemos asks all our community to exist harmoniously and embrace the richness of diversity. Users must not post offensive content against any ethnicity, race, gender identity, sexual orientation or religion.

    Yeemos asks that you DO NOT call out, name drop or shame other users that have interacted with you in a manner which makes you uncomfortable. Privacy features such as the ability to Block a user or set your account to Private are at your disposal and should be used in such circumstances. If you still feel that a user is deliberately spamming or harassing you, the in-app reporting system is the correct way to ensure this matter is brought to our immediate attention.

    Abide by the law

    Content posted on Yeemos MUST NOT:

  • Incite illegal behavior or violence
  • Support or encourage terrorism or hate groups
  • Share material that sexually exploits minors
  • Constitute fraudulent activity, including obtaining or attempting to obtain financial advantage or private information by deception

    • Other important stuff that won't be tolerated

  • Call out posts or name dropping that is likely to lead to an escalation of behavior that is detrimental to the community. Users that breach this will face an immediate ban
  • Posts that contain sexually explicit content must be flagged with the text 'NSFW' in the body of the posts. NSFW stands for Not Safe For Work and is a common way of describing content that is sexually explicit in nature
  • Spam or other forms of solicitation
  • Post gore or pornographic material
  • Automated or programmatic methods of creating Yeemos accounts, posting content or boosting your interactive button numbers
  • Username hoarding. Owning a few active accounts for various purposes is welcomed however registering and saving usernames in large volumes will not be tolerated. Yeemos reserves the right to reclaim usernames on behalf of businesses or individuals that hold legal claim or trademark on those usernames. Accounts using business names and/or logos to mislead others may be permanently suspended.
  • Respect our systems - do not interfere with or disrupt the Site or the servers or networks connected to the Site
